NOTE #1234 - Philosophy of Design

Neo-Brutalism in Digital Design

Neo-brutalism represents a bold departure from the soft, gradient-heavy design trends of recent years. It embraces:

  • Bold, thick borders that create strong visual hierarchy
  • High contrast color schemes with intentional clashing
  • Geometric shapes and asymmetrical layouts
  • Typography as a design element, not just text

This aesthetic works particularly well for Zettl because it maintains minimalism while adding visual interest. The stark borders and bold colors help users quickly identify different sections and navigate complex information structures.

Benefits for Knowledge Management

The visual distinctiveness of neo-brutalist design aids in memory formation and recall. When each note or section has a unique visual treatment, it becomes easier to remember where information is stored.
